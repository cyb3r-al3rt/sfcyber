Phishing Resilience Assessment

Test your team's ability to recognize phishing attacks and implement email security controls to prevent credential theft.

Who This Is For

Small and mid-sized businesses in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and across India that rely on email for daily operations and need to protect against:

  • Business email compromise (BEC)
  • Credential phishing and account takeovers
  • Spear-phishing targeting executives or finance teams
  • Fake vendor invoices and wire-transfer fraud

What You Get

1. Simulated Phishing Campaign

We send realistic (but safe) phishing emails to your team to measure click rates, credential submission rates, and reporting behavior.

2. Email Security Audit

We check your domain's DMARC, SPF, and DKIM records to ensure you have proper email authentication and prevent spoofing.

3. Training Recommendations

Customized guidance on how to train your team to recognize and report phishing attempts, aligned with CISA best practices.

4. Remediation Action Plan

Clear, step-by-step instructions to fix identified gaps and reduce your phishing risk immediately.

Process & Timeline

  1. Day 1: Discovery call to understand your email environment and team size
  2. Days 2-3: Simulated phishing campaign execution
  3. Days 4-5: Email security audit and results analysis
  4. Days 6-7: Report delivery and remediation planning call

Total timeline: 5-7 business days from kickoff to final report delivery.

Pricing

Investment starts at ₹25,000 for teams up to 25 users. Pricing scales based on team size and complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my team know this is a test?

We recommend telling leadership and IT that a phishing test is coming, but keeping the broader team unaware for accurate results.

What if our team performs poorly?

That's the point of the assessment. We'll provide clear training and technical recommendations to improve resilience quickly.

Do you provide ongoing phishing simulation?

Yes, we can set up recurring campaigns on a monthly or quarterly basis as part of an ongoing advisory engagement.
