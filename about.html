About SF Cyber Advisory

Practical cybersecurity consulting for businesses in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai.

Who We Help

We work with small and mid-sized businesses across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and India that need affordable, actionable cybersecurity guidance without the enterprise consulting price tag.

Our clients include:

  • Accounting and professional services firms
  • Marketing and creative agencies
  • Healthcare and dental practices
  • Law firms and legal practices
  • E-commerce and retail businesses
  • Financial advisors and consultancies

How We're Different

Most cybersecurity firms focus on large enterprises with complex compliance requirements. We focus on practical, high-impact security improvements for SMBs:

  • Fast delivery: Most assessments completed in 3-7 days
  • Clear language: No jargon—just actionable advice
  • Focused scope: We solve specific problems, not endless "maturity assessments"
  • Affordable pricing: Fixed-price engagements designed for SMB budgets

Our Approach

Every engagement starts with a discovery call to understand your specific pain points. We then recommend the highest-impact service to solve your immediate problem—not a year-long roadmap you'll never execute.

Our methodology is grounded in industry-standard frameworks like OWASP, NIST, and CISA guidance, adapted for the realities of small business budgets and timelines.
