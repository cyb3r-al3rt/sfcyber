Wireless Network Audit

Ensure your Wi-Fi isn't a welcome mat for data thieves.

Who This Is For

  • Retail stores and restaurants with customer Wi-Fi
  • Offices with guest wireless networks
  • Co-working spaces and hospitality businesses
  • Any company offering free public Wi-Fi

What We Check

Encryption Strength

Verify WPA2/WPA3 usage and identify weak WEP/WPA networks that can be cracked in minutes.

Rogue Access Points

Detect unauthorized routers or employee-installed hotspots that bypass your security.

Guest Network Isolation

Ensure guest Wi-Fi is properly segregated from business systems and sensitive data.

Configuration Hardening

Recommendations to disable WPS, optimize security settings, and implement MAC filtering where appropriate.

Timeline & Pricing

Timeline: 3-5 business days

Investment: ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 depending on number of locations and access points
